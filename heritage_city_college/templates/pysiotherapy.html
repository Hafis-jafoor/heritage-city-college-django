Bachelor of Physiotherapy

The on-campus physiotherapy clinic, known for its excellent facilitation, serves as the flagship of the physiotherapy department. Equipped with modern tools, it provides students with essential hands-on clinical experience crucial for their future as physiotherapists. This enhances their skills and deepens their understanding of each piece of equipment's features and usage, ultimately benefiting the patients they will serve in the future.

Opportunities

The need for physiotherapists is experiencing rapid growth, given their integral role in both diagnosis and treatment. Job prospects in the field encompass a range of roles including,

  • Independent Consultants
  • Administrators
  • Specialists
  • Researchers
  • Educators
